despite a diagnosis of CaP. Furthermore, our data demonstrate a
lower rate of CaP recurrence in men on TRT than the published
recurrence rate for men with CaP not treated with TRT. However,
in the continued absence of randomized controlled trial data, TRT
in patients with CaP should be undertaken with appropriate
follow-up.
